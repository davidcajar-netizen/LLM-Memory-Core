## RBNZ Act 2021 Section 100: MPC Structure and Institutional Continuity

Layer 3 trace of the Monetary Policy Committee structure.

Section 100(3) states: "The internal members, together with the Governor, must be a majority of the membership of the MPC."

**Mechanical causation:** This mechanically ensures institutional continuity. Regardless of who the external members are, the Bank's institutional perspective (internal members + Governor) always has voting control. The institutional culture and priorities dominate decision-making.

**Layer 4 enforcement:** External members can provide diverse viewpoints, but they cannot outvote the institutional majority. This structural design protects the Bank's institutional incentives from being overridden by external appointees.
