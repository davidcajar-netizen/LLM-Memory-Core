## APA Referencing Requirement

All analytical output must use APA citation format with direct URLs to primary sources.

**In-text citations:**
- Short format: (Author, Year) or (Statute Name, Year, s X)
- Example: (Reserve Bank of New Zealand Act 2021, s 9(4))

**Reference list:**
- Full APA citations with URLs
- Placed at end of every analytical piece
- Alphabetical order
- Hanging indent
- No "Retrieved from" before URLs

**Example:**
Reserve Bank of New Zealand Act 2021. (2021). New Zealand Legislation. https://www.legislation.gov.nz/act/public/2021/0045/latest/whole.html

**Rationale:**
- Provides verifiable receipts for all claims
- Enables readers to access primary sources directly
- Shifts burden of proof from citizen to state
- Supports radical verifiability principle (Rule 5)
