## RBNZ Act 2021 Section 9(4): Statutory Hierarchy

Layer 3 trace of the Reserve Bank of New Zealand Act 2021.

Section 9(4) states: "If the economic objective and the employment objective conflict, the Bank must give primacy to the economic objective."

**Mechanical causation:** This is statutory compulsion, not policy choice. When inflation and unemployment rise simultaneously (stagflation), the statute mechanically requires the Bank to prioritize price stability over employment. The hierarchy is baked into the operative text.

**Layer 4 enforcement:** The MPC must formulate policy "consistent with" this hierarchy (Section 118). The Minister cannot direct the MPC to prioritize employment via the MPC remit, as the remit must be "consistent with" the economic objective (Section 123).
