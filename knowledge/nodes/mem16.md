# Health Funding Architecture: General Appropriation vs. Hypothecated Levy

**Statutory Mechanism:** 
*Pae Ora (Healthy Futures) Act 2022* (funding Health New Zealand through general Crown appropriations) compared to Australia's hypothecated 3% Medicare Levy.

**Layer 3 Mechanical Causation:** 
The Australian system uses a hypothecated levy, effectively putting money in a specific statutory jar labeled exclusively for health. In New Zealand, the *Pae Ora (Healthy Futures) Act 2022* funds the health system through general Crown appropriations. All tax revenue is thrown into one giant consolidated fund, and health funding is a residual claimant on that fund. 

**Layer 4 Enforcement Reality:** 
There is no statutory architecture in the *Pae Ora Act* to create or enforce a hypothecated levy. Because health funding competes with all other government expenditures in the general pool, it is chronically underfunded and subject to annual political bargaining. The system functions exactly as designed: without a dedicated, ring-fenced revenue stream, the health system is perpetually starved of capital, relying on whatever is left over after other statutory priorities are met.
