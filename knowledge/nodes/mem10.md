## RBNZ Act 2021 Sections 171 & 239: Double Unaccountability

Layer 4 trace of the enforcement and accountability mechanics.

**Section 171 (Independence Safeguard):** Mechanically prevents ministerial direction on specific cases. The institution is bolted to the floor with statutory independence.

**Section 239 (Annual Reporting):** Requires transparency (reporting on affairs of the Bank) but has no enforcement mechanism for failing to achieve statutory objectives.

**Mechanical reality:** The Act creates clear objectives but weak enforcement. The Governor can only be removed for "just cause" (misconduct, neglect), not for policy failure.

This creates a double layer of unaccountability:
1. **The institution is unaccountable** (protected from political interference, no penalty for failing objectives).
2. **The person in the role is unaccountable** (cannot be removed for policy failure).

You cannot "exchange the guy" because the new person is constrained by the same statutory architecture.
