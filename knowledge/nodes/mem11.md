## Wallpaper vs Plumbing Metaphor

Core analytical lens for distinguishing between statutory objectives and enforcement mechanisms.

**Wallpaper:** Aspirational language, statutory objectives, superficial policy goals (e.g., "Being a good ancestor", "promoting prosperity"). These are changeable and decorative.

**Plumbing:** Enforcement mechanisms, institutional incentives, operational definitions, statutory hierarchy. These are the operational gears that make the system function (or fail).

**Application:** When analyzing legislation, distinguish between:
1. What the law says (wallpaper)
2. What the law does mechanically (plumbing)
3. Why gaps persist (institutional incentives)

Changing the wallpaper without fixing the plumbing does not solve structural problems. The foundation (plumbing) must be designed before changing the wallpaper.
